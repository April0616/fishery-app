Fishery App Instructions

This is a fishery game with 3 players.

The default parameters are as follows: intrinsic_growth_rate: {{ this.SESSION_CONFIGS.intrinsic_growth_rate }}, strength_of_density_regulation: {{ this.SESSION_CONFIGS.strength_of_density_regulation }}, sustainable_yield: {{ this.SESSION_CONFIGS.sustainable_yield }}, starting_fish_count: {{ this.SESSION_CONFIGS.starting_fish_count }}, You are a fisherman. In the beginning, there are 150 fish in the fishery. Every year your friends and you can catch some fish by choosing the number of fish you want to catch.
